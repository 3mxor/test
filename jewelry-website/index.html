Our Story

Crafted with Purpose

A heritage of fine jewelry, a commitment to the future.

Founded on Beauty,
Sustained by Craft

Lumière was born in Paris in 1994 from a single belief: that jewelry should be as enduring as the love it represents. Our founder, Isabelle Marchand, trained under master goldsmiths in the Marais district before opening her first atelier on the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.

Three decades later, every Lumière piece is still handcrafted in our Paris studio using the same traditional techniques — wax carving, hand-setting, and meticulous finishing — combined with the finest ethically sourced gemstones from around the world.

We believe in slow jewelry. In buying less, but buying beautifully. In pieces that are passed down through generations rather than discarded with seasons.

Est. 1994

Our Pillars

What We Stand For

Ethical Sourcing

Every gemstone is conflict-free and traceable. We partner only with mines and suppliers who meet the highest ethical and environmental standards.

Master Craftsmanship

Our artisans spend years mastering traditional goldsmithing techniques. Each piece passes through more than forty pairs of hands before it reaches yours.

Timeless Design

We reject seasonal trends in favour of designs that will be cherished for a lifetime — and beyond. Classic proportions, refined silhouettes, lasting beauty.

Sustainable Practice

We use recycled gold in our settings, minimize waste through precision casting, and offset all carbon emissions from shipping.
